TEAS and their HISTORY

History of tea

There are legends about how exactly people found tea in different countries of the world.

There are especially many stories about this plant in China, Japan and India.

According to one of them, Indian, the tea bush was found by Badhidharma during his travels in China.

The Japanese claim that the tea bush grew from the eyelids of Prince Daruma, which he cut and threw on the ground to keep himself awake during meditation.

By the way, it is for this reason that tea has tonic properties and helps fight fatigue and drowsiness.

According to the same legend, these properties of the drink were discovered by the students of Prince Daruma by brewing the leaves of an unusual bush that had grown.
